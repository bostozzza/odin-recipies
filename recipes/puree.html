Mashed potatoes recipe

>

Description

Delicious mashed potatoes, great as a side dish for any type of main course

The whole process should take no more than 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes - 200 g
  • Butter - 20 g
  • Milk - 50 ml
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Parsley - 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and boil them for 15 minutes in salted water. Remove the water and leave the potatoes in the pot to rest for approx. 5 minutes.
  2. Add the butter and wait for it to melt. Then pour milk into the pot.
  3. Smash the potatoes with a dedicated potato masher for approx. 2-3 minutes. If you don't have one, you can use simple kitchen fork.
  4. Serve immediately after preparing, while still very hot. If you want, you can finish with some seasoning - a sprinkle of salt and some parsley for additional taste.

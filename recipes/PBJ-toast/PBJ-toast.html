PBJ Toast recipes

Description

Best PBJ Toast sandwiches ever!

Choose the jam yourself!

However, the best are strawberry and blueberry jams ;)

Ingredients

  • Toast bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Your favourite jam

Steps

  1. Put the bread into the toaster for 3 minutes
  2. Meanwhile, contemplate which jam you choose
  3. Once the toasts are ready, put some jam and peanut butter on them in equal amounts
  4. Bon appetit!

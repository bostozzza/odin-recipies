Hot-Dog recipe

Description

Beautiful hot-dog with a giant sausage

Best served hot with ketchup and mustard

Ingredients

  • Hot-Dog bun
  • Sausage
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Fried onion

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 220Â°C. When ready, put two hot-dog buns in for 10 minutes
  2. Meanwhile, boil the sausages for 10 minutes
  3. When both buns and sausages are ready, stick the giant sausage into the warm bun
  4. Season with some ketchup, mustard nad fried onion
  5. Serve immediately after preparing
